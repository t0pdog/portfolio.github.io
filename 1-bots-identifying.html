NLP task - Identifying bots among annotators

This project aims to address the challenge of identifying data that has been annotated by bots. Since identifying untrustworthy annotators relies on their responses, the goal is to develop a solution that effectively filters out such bot-generated annotations, enhancing the overall quality and reliability of the annotated data.
